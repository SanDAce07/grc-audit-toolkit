## Access Control — Sample Testing

### Control: User Access Reviews Performed Quarterly

**Control Description:** Management performs quarterly access reviews for all in-scope systems to ensure access is appropriate and least privilege is maintained.

**Test Procedure:**
1. Obtain access review documentation for all quarters in audit period
2. Verify reviews were completed timely (within 30 days of quarter end)
3. Select a documented sample of users and verify access is appropriate
4. Verify terminated users were removed within 24 hours
